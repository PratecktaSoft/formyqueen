✦   A Letter Written in Love   ✦

Read My Heart ❤️

Because some feelings are too deep for just words…

My Dearest Divya,

There are moments in life when you meet someone and quietly realize — the world just became a better place. You are that moment for me. Every single day.

You didn't just walk into my life, Divya. You walked in and rearranged everything — turned my ordinary into extraordinary, and made me believe I deserved something beautiful.

I love the way you laugh when something genuinely surprises you. I love how you care so deeply — about people, about little things, about the kind of world you want to live in. That depth in you? It pulled me in the moment I saw it.

· · · ✦ · · ·

Maybe I haven't always found the right words. Maybe there were evenings I stayed quiet when I should have spoken. But silence was never distance — it was just me, holding so much for you that I didn't know where to begin.

I want you to know this: in every version of every future I've ever imagined, you are there. Not because I need you to complete me — but because everything feels more alive when you're part of it.

You are not just the love of my life. You are the reason I want to be the best version of myself — someone worthy of standing beside you.

So on the days you feel unseen, come back to this page. Because I built it for you — not with tools and code, but with every feeling I've been carrying quietly in my chest.

No matter how many seasons change, one thing will always stay the same: my heart has your name written in it — in ink that never fades. 💛

· · · ✦ · · ·

Always & without question

Forever Yours ✨
— written from the truest corner of my heart
